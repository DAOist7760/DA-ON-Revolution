## What DA·ON Is

DA·ON (Dynamic-Allocation Organic-Network) is a governance protocol for purposeful human Collectives that want to coordinate action without hierarchy.

It is built on a single mathematically expressible axiom — the **ARA Principle**:

> *Authority, Responsibility, and Accountability are inextricably related in every act of delegation. When any element of this mapping collapses — hierarchy emerges by default.*

DA·ON makes the mapping explicit, visible, and mutually agreed upon before any work begins. When the mapping holds, hierarchy cannot form. When hierarchy cannot form, the Collective can breathe.

## The Problem We Are Solving

Three conditions are always present when humans cooperate spontaneously without domination:

1. **No inferred hierarchy** — no one whose word carries more weight by position alone
2. **A commonly held belief** — a shared purpose that everyone feels pulling in the same direction
3. **Social permission and proof** — someone who demonstrates movement, converting shared passion into conviction

When any one of these three conditions is lost, the Collective immediately dissolves.

DA·ON is the technical infrastructure that *preserves condition 1* while conditions 2 and 3 are held by the humans in the Collective.

The 3–7 year Utopian-vision → Devastating-dissolution cycle observed across Intentional Communities worldwide is the empirical evidence of what happens without this infrastructure. DA·ON is the intervention.
