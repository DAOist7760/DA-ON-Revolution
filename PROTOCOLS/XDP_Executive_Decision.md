## What the XDP Is

In the real world, underlying assumptions upon which contracts
were predicated will be found in error, circumstances will change,
or new opportunities will present themselves.

When any member first **SENSES** a *"What do we do NOW?"* question
that extends beyond their ADP-License and may impact others on
the Mission-Tree, they formally declare an **XDP-Question.**

That declaration **spontaneously creates** an Executive Body
licensed to make only the specific decision demanded by the
specific triggering question. Upon completion, the Body is
**automatically dissolved.**

---

## Relationship to the ADP

| Protocol | Governs | Triggered by |
|---|---|---|
| **ADP** | Steady-state — who does what | Mission initiation · new delegation need |
| **XDP** | Dynamic-state — what we do when reality changes | Any member sensing a WHAT question beyond their license |

Together they cover the **complete governance lifecycle**
of a purposeful Collective.

## The Three Automated Phases

### Phase 1 — SCOPING

**Initiating the XDP:**

State the XDP-Question as a **factual contrast:**
- What was assumed (or anticipated)
- What is found to be the real-world case
- Why it matters (the implication)

This brief declaration becomes the query in an automated search
from the Root of the Mission-Tree through every current delegation
for anyone whose License is likely to be affected.

**Scope determination:**
- Everyone whose ADP-License is impacted = **required to participate**
- XDP-Question posted publicly = **anyone with valid interest may apply**
- Subject-matter expertise gaps = **Executive Body may intentionally recruit beyond current Delegates** (this itself requires Consensus)

*Note: AI assists the Mission-Tree search — AI does not govern inclusion.*

---

### Phase 2 — DELIBERATION

Deliberate distillation of the full range of concerns, perspectives,
and values down to a single actionable path forward.

**The Deliberation Meta-Cycle (DMC):**

| Step | Name | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | Consideration | Collection of raw data, perspectives, and opinions |
| **2** | Analysis | Mapping the ideosphere — reducing Raws to relational patterns |
| **3** | Differentiation | Cataloging ideas as alternatives with Pro/Con lists |
| **4** | Weighting | Each member assigns 0–5 value to each element with supporting commentary |

**Algorithm processing:**

Upon completion of Step 4, an algorithm processes the weights
to reach for coalescence. Two complementary algorithm flavors
are employed per iteration:

- **Aggregation algorithms** — designed to aggregate toward Consensus
- **Differentiation algorithms** — designed to differentiate Options

The pair complement each other. The 0–5 weight structure makes
all options mathematically comparable across members.

**Natural disambiguation:**

In the commentary associated with Weights, people naturally
attempt to justify their evaluations. Ambiguities in language
surface here and are resolved in the next iteration.
Value-system differences become visible and discussable.

**The Catch-22 Paradox:**

When diametrically opposed alternatives are discovered,
two resolutions exist:
1. Parallel actions moving forward simultaneously
2. Dissolving the Perceived-Paradox by rooting to the common
   value that ties the two options together *(a protocol exists for this)*

*Most questions reach actionable Consensus within three DMC iterations.*

---

### Phase 3 — CONSENSUS

**Setting the Appropriate-Threshold:**

Before initiating the DMC, three parameters must be set:

| Parameter | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Appropriate-Threshold** | The consensus level required to complete the XDP — user-set, varies by question type and context |
| **Appropriate-Toolset** | The DMC methodologies chosen for this specific question |
| **Appropriate-Heuristics** | All relevant Precedence from the Collective's history, assembled before deliberation begins |

**Completion:**

When the Threshold is reached:
1. The accumulated Data & Decision are recorded in the **Precedence-Record**
2. The Executive Body is **automatically dissolved**
3. The Heuristic Wrapper grows — the Collective's accumulated wisdom increases

**The Heuristic Wrapper:**

Because no Executive-Decision-Authority exists outside of sensing
an XDP-Question, the XDP is a purely Heuristic function.
All prior decisions are available as Precedence for future questions.
The Collective learns from itself over time.

---

## MVP Implementation Note

In the MVP phase, the DMC algorithm processing is replaced by
**human facilitation.** The four-step structure remains identical.
The algorithm automation is added in Phase 2 of the development roadmap.

This means: the XDP can be operated today, manually, using
GitHub Issues as the deliberation surface. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](../../CONTRIBUTING.md)
for how we are currently doing this.
