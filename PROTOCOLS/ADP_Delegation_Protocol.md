## The Axiomatic Foundation

DA·ON is the product of instantiating Purposeful-Relations between
Collective-Members in accord with the **ARA Delegation Principle** —
one of the 12 axiomatic mechanisms of the AABE Social-Architecture.

### The ARA Axiom

**D = (A, R, Ac)**

Where:
- **A** = Authority — the scope of license to act on behalf of the Delegator
- **R** = Responsibility — the defined Work-Product with measurable outcomes (KPIs)
- **Ac** = Accountability — dual measurement: Quantity (Delegator-measured) and Quality (Peer-measured)

**Delegation Integrity Condition:**

A Delegation **D** is integrity-preserving if and only if:

**∀ D : A ≠ ∅ ∧ R ≠ ∅ ∧ Ac ≠ ∅**

*For every Delegation: Authority, Responsibility, and Accountability
must all be non-empty. None can be absent or undefined.*

**The Collapse Theorem:**

| If this element is absent | Then this emerges |
|---|---|
| A = ∅ (undefined Authority) | Informal power — hierarchy |
| R = ∅ (undefined Responsibility) | Role ambiguity — hierarchy |
| Ac = ∅ (undefined Accountability) | Social pressure — hierarchy |

**∃ x ∈ {A, R, Ac} : x = ∅ ⟹ H**

*If any single element of the ARA triple is empty,
hierarchy emerges by default.*

## The Two Domains of Authority — Sacred Boundary

| | Executive-Authority | Strategic-Authority |
|---|---|---|
| **Question** | WHAT gets done? | HOW it gets done? |
| **Held by** | The Collective (retained by Delegator) | The Delegate (received via License) |
| **Direction** | Flows toward the Root — collective decision | Flows toward the Leaves — individual autonomy |
| **Accountability** | Quantity: volume / time (KPI) | Quality: peer-to-peer by Receiver |
| **If crossed** | Collective-Organism dies | Autonomous behavior conditions lost |

## The Gestation Sequence

Before DA·ON can be instantiated, an AABE-Lifeform must be
gestated through emotional alignment of the founding members:

ETHOS → CAUSE → VISION → MISSION → ROOT-MISSION → DA·ON


**Minimum viable seed:** For simple use cases (family reunion,
small project team), only the **Purposeful Mission** is required.
The full Culturing sequence is required only for Sustainable
Community Collectives.

## Creating an ADP-License — The Six Elements

Every delegation requires these six elements, negotiated live
(audio minimum; video preferred) between three parties:
the **Delegator**, the **Delegate**, and the **Receiver of the Work-Product**.

The live negotiation is not optional — it is the relational act
that creates the Trustful Space. The six-element form is the
official record of that live relationship.

| Element | Description |
|---|---|
| **1. The Need** | Generalized description of the missing resource (Skills, Materials, Capital) — posted as a marketplace invitation |
| **2. The Work-Product** | Unambiguous description of the Delegator's intended result — WHAT gets done, not HOW |
| **3. Quantity (KPIs)** | Clearly defined measurements marking progress over time — Delegator Accountability |
| **4. Quality-Standard** | Minimum and maximum quality requirements — negotiated by the three parties; accumulates into an Internal Library over time |
| **5. Receiver of Work-Product** | The person who measures Quality-Accountability peer-to-peer — identified before work begins |
| **6. Resources & Remuneration** | Allocation of the Commons sufficient to support fulfillment |

## How the Mission-Tree Grows

Each ADP-License becomes a Node on the Mission-Tree.
The Delegate receives both the Responsibility for their domain
AND the Executive-Authority to delegate further within it.

ROOT-MISSION

.  └── Delegation 1 (John) ── Responsibility A
  
.  └── Delegation 2 (Sally) ── Responsibility B
  
.  └── Delegation 3 (Alice) ── Responsibility C
  
.  └── [continues organically...]


**The Organic Network Theorem:**

The Mission-Tree T grows by recursive application of
integrity-preserving delegations:

**T = {D₀, D₁, D₂, ... Dₙ}**

Where each **Dᵢ = (Aᵢ, Rᵢ, Acᵢ)** satisfies the Delegation
Integrity Condition, and each Dᵢ is derived from the
authority domain of its parent Dᵢ₋₁.

**Result:** Authority distributes organically outward without
concentration. No node can accumulate authority beyond its
licensed domain. Hierarchy cannot form.
